Smart & Easy Ration Services

Smart Ration is a digital public distribution system that allows citizens to check stock, book ration items, view monthly quota and receive government updates online. No long queues, fast service, and complete transparency for everyone.

Vision

Transparent and digital ration distribution for every citizen.

Mission

Provide quick, queue-free and easy access to ration services.

Goal

Ensure food security using smart technology and automation.
