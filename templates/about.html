Smart Ration System

Smart Ration System is a digital platform developed to modernize the traditional Public Distribution System (PDS). It allows citizens to check stock availability, submit complaints, view government notices, and access ration services online in a transparent and efficient way. This system reduces manual work and improves the management of ration distribution through a centralized database.

🎯 Our Goal

The goal of the Smart Ration System is to improve transparency and efficiency in the ration distribution process. It aims to reduce corruption, prevent stock mismanagement, and minimize long queues at ration shops. By providing online access to information, the system ensures fair distribution of essential commodities to all eligible card holders.

Services Provided

📦 Stock Availability

Customers can check available ration items and quantities in real time before visiting the shop.

📢 Government Notices

Displays latest announcements, price changes, and government schemes related to ration distribution.

📝 Complaint System

Customers can report issues such as stock shortage, poor quality items, or technical problems.

👤 Card Holder Management

The admin can manage ration card holder information including card type, members, and mobile details.

Why Smart Ration is Different?

Traditional ration shops often face problems such as long queues, lack of transparency, and uncertainty about stock availability. The Smart Ration System solves these issues by providing digital records and online information access. Customers can check stock availability before visiting the shop and receive updates instantly, which improves efficiency and customer satisfaction.

⭐ Key Features

  • ✔ Real-time stock monitoring
  • ✔ Digital complaint submission
  • ✔ Automatic date and time recording
  • ✔ Shop and government notices displayed separately
  • ✔ Admin dashboard for managing users and stock
  • ✔ Secure database storage using MySQL
